About Me

As a front-end developer, I am passionate about creating beautiful and intuitive user-friendly websites that provide a great user experience. Though I may not have prior work experience in the field, my dedication to learning and improving my skills has allowed me to create various projects to showcase my abilities.

My journey into coding began by accident when I took a programing course, and I quickly realized it aligned with my love for problem-solving and creating things. Since then I have immersed myself in the world of web development, learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript and supporting technologies to create visually appealing and easy to use websites. I am continuously expanding my knowledge and am eager to learn and grow.

In my free time I enjoy pursuing my hobbies of archery and canoeing, archery requires focus and canoeing requires persistence, both of which are required by coding. I am excited to continue exploring new challenges and opportunities in the world of front-end development and to collaborate with other passionate individuals to create smooth and memorable experiences for users.
